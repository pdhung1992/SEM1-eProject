The 'Smallest, Savviest' Black Friday Ever

On Black Friday last year, 40 items sold every second on DigiAuctions VN and a whopping 90% of those purchases were from a small business. Today, DigiAuctions VN has announced that they are prepping for its ‘smallest’ Black Friday yet, with 66% of the VN planning to use this day to purchase Christmas gifts from small businesses.

Since the pandemic, shoppers are not only thinking more about what kind of businesses they spend their pounds with, but also how they make more money to fund new purchases, with 30% of the VN planning on selling unwanted items ahead of this Black Friday.
